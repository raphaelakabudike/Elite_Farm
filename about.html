About Elite Farm

Committed to sustainable farming and delivering premium quality poultry products since 2010

Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Excellence

Elite Farm started in 2010 with a simple vision: to provide fresh, high-quality poultry products while maintaining the highest standards of animal welfare and environmental sustainability.

What began as a small family operation with just 500 birds has grown into one of the region's most trusted poultry suppliers, serving thousands of satisfied customers across Ghana.

Today, we operate a modern facility with over 10,000 birds, state-of-the-art equipment, and a dedicated team committed to excellence in every aspect of our operation.

Our Journey

Growing Together

Key milestones in our journey to becoming a trusted poultry supplier.

2010

The Beginning

Started with 500 layer hens on a small family farm, focusing on fresh egg production for local markets.

2013

Expansion

Expanded operations to include broiler production and increased capacity to 3,000 birds.

2016

Modernization

Invested in modern facilities and equipment, implementing automated feeding and climate control systems.

2020

Certification

Achieved organic certification and implemented comprehensive quality management systems.

2024

Today

Operating at full capacity with 10,000+ birds, serving customers across Ghana with premium products.
