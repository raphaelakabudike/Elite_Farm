Refund Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Quality Guarantee

We stand behind the quality of our products 100%. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are available for:

  • Products that don't meet our quality standards
  • Damaged products upon delivery
  • Incorrect items delivered
  • Missing items from your order

Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Contact us within 24 hours of delivery
  2. Provide your order number and photos of the issue
  3. Our team will review and respond within 24 hours
  4. Approved refunds are processed within 3-5 business days

Non-Refundable Items

Due to the perishable nature of our products, we cannot accept returns for change of mind or personal preference.
